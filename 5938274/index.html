About Me

I'm an ETH Zürich graduate specializing in designing, developing, and deploying scalable, production-grade systems across NLP, computer vision, and finance. My experience spans from reducing system latency by 65% to building full-stack automated portfolio management platforms that actually make money (a surprisingly rare skill in fintech).

I have a strong track record of delivering high-impact, reliable software under tight deadlines—because nothing teaches you system optimization quite like a trading algorithm that needs to respond in milliseconds. My work bridges theoretical computer science with practical applications, from CI/CD pipeline integration to MLOps, ensuring that brilliant algorithms actually work when users need them most.
